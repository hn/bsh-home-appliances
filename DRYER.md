## Hardware

### Control board EPT58131

The control board ([PCB top](photos/bsh-EPT58131-pcb-top.jpg) [PCB bottom](photos/bsh-EPT58131-pcb-bottom.jpg) [Enclosure](photos/bsh-EPT58131-enclosure.jpg))
are equipped with an [RENESAS R5F104JHA MCU](https://www.renesas.com/en/products/microcontrollers-microprocessors/rl78-low-power-8-16-bit-mcus/rl78g14-low-power-high-function-general-purpose-microcontrollers-motor-control-industrial-and-metering)
(RL78/G14 CISC CPU core, 32pin, 128k flash ROM, 8kb Data flash, 16kb RAM, [datasheet](https://www.renesas.com/en/document/dst/rl78g14-data-sheet?r=1054296)).

The board has [three D-Bus connectors](photos/bsh-EPT58131-pcb-top.jpg). The top one is doubled in three traces at the top of the PCB.
